About

Data Science is the amalgamation of all the tech classes I took in college. I started my journey in the realm of Physics and creating rocket engine models in Python. I moved on from that into the world of statistical wrangling and modeling with R. Finally, everything was brought together when my courses in Data Science merged those areas together in such a perfect way.

Within the realm of Data Science, I learned quickly that just looking at numbers wasn't going to get me anywhere. Answers needed to be pulled from the messy junk that some data tables can find themselves in. I learned how to wrangle data into a workable space, I found useful ways to visualize said data so anybody reading it could understand the answers I found, and I discovered that most any question can be answered if enough of the proper data is present.
